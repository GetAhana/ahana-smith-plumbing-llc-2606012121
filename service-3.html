Drains

Fast drain cleaning and sewer services you can count on

Slow kitchen drains and bathroom backups are warnings your lines need clearing. We cable or hydro-jet clogs, use camera follow-up when needed, show you video findings, and explain what caused the blockage so you can avoid it next time.

Drain service standards we maintain

  • HD camera inspection with video you keep for your records
  • Cabling for minor clogs; hydro-jetting for buildup and roots
  • Clear written findings explaining blockage type and location
  • Main-line cleanouts tested and verified for flow

Over 300 drain and sewer jobs cleared in Phoenix, Scottsdale, and surrounding areas—roots, grease, and backed-up toilets handled with same-day response.

How we handle Drains

Our drain cleaning process uses the right tool for each clog type.

  • Describe what is draining slowly — Is it one fixture or the whole house? When did it start? We ask these questions to decide whether you need a local line cleared or a main-line camera scope.
  • We scope and identify the clog — For main-line issues, we run HD camera through the sewer to show you the blockage type—grease, roots, offset, or broken section—and discuss options.
  • Cable or hydro-jet and verify — We clear the line with the right tool, run water to confirm flow, and explain what caused the clog so you can prevent it repeating.
When to call us

These warning signs mean your drains need professional attention.

  • Multiple drains in the house slow or backing up at once
  • Gurgling sounds from drains, especially when toilet flushes
  • Water pooling or soft ground in the yard near the sewer line
  • Sewage smell from indoor drains or outdoor access points
  • Recurring kitchen or bathroom clogs within weeks of clearing
